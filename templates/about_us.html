About Us

Welcome to Fowl Disease Detection!

We are a team of four final-year Computer Science and Engineering students dedicated to improving poultry health through innovative technology. Our mission is to empower poultry owners with an easy-to-use web platform for detecting diseases in hens based on their symptoms, enhancing flock well-being and supporting farmers.

Our Values

Compassion

The health of animals is at the heart of our project, and we strive to provide effective solutions.

Innovation

We believe in leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

Collaboration

Working together as a team and with our future users is key to our success.

Our Inspiration

Noticing a lack of accessible resources for poultry owners facing health issues, we combined our passion for technology and animal health to create a solution. Our commitment is to make poultry farming more efficient and sustainable.

What We Offer

Our platform allows poultry owners to input symptoms observed in their hens and receive immediate suggestions for potential diseases and remedies. By providing this vital information, we aim to reduce the time and stress associated with disease management in poultry farming.

Community Impact

We understand the importance of healthy livestock to both farmers and communities. Our project not only seeks to enhance poultry health but also to contribute to sustainable farming practices that benefit everyone. Thank you for visiting our page! We're excited to share our project with you, and we look forward to helping poultry owners keep their hens healthy. If you'd like to learn more or get involved, feel free to reach out!
